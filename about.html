About me

Here's a brief history of who I am, what I do, and why we should be friends: my name is George Akritidis, I was born in Thessaloniki and I live in this city ever since. I have a degree in Informatics and currectly I design and develop web pages. For me, designing and developing go hand in hand. Without one, the other wouldn't exist. What describes me the most is that I am a teamplayer who strongly values collaboration within a team. When my face isn't pressed against my computer screen I find myself working out in the gym, riding my bike, and watching movies.

Beside web design I have worked also as a lifeguard here in Greece. The job of a lifeguard entails a lot of responsibility and I have to tell you that my experiences affected my way of thinking and made me look the world differently. Furthermore, I had the chance to meet numerous people from all around the world.

Lately, I've been focusing on creating responsive web pages that adjust in every media device. In addition, I study programming languages for android and ios applications because I believe that smartphones will expand and inflitrate even more in our lifes. Afterall, software is the staging ground for the future.

If you have any questions me you can e-mail me on geoakr84@pathfinder.gr . If you don't have any questions you can view my facebook account or if you are the kind of person who prefers things square I’m now on Instagram .
